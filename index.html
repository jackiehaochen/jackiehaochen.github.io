Biography

I have finished my PhD study in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Heng and Prof. Jack C. Y. Cheng. Previously, I received the B.Eng. in School of Instrumentation Science and Opto-electronics Engineering from Beihang University (BUAA).

Research Interests

Health Informatics, Medical Image Computing, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, etc.
